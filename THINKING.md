# THINKING DOCUMENT

## 1. Why is expense trend analysis important in accounting software?
Expense trend analysis is essential in accounting systems because it enables businesses to monitor financial performance and maintain cost control.
It helps in:
- Budget planning and forecasting  
- Identifying abnormal or suspicious spending patterns  
- Monitoring GST liability and compliance  
- Vendor spending evaluation  
- Cash flow management  
By analyzing trends over time, businesses can make informed financial decisions and ensure operational efficiency.

---

## 2. What could be misleading if data quality is poor?
Poor data quality can significantly distort financial insights.
Examples include:
- Incorrect invoice dates misrepresenting monthly trends  
- Duplicate invoices inflating total expense  
- Incorrect GST calculations affecting tax reporting  
- Misclassified expense categories misleading cost analysis  
- Missing values reducing reliability of insights  

Accurate, structured, and validated data is essential for meaningful financial analysis and decision-making.
